About The Role

We are seeking a highly motivated Human Resources Intern with a passion for data analytics and a keen interest in office culture. In this role, you will have the opportunity to combine your analytical skills with your understanding of human behavior to drive meaningful insights and initiatives within our HR department.

Some of the key responsibilities you should expect are the following:

Data Analysis: Utilize data analytics tools and techniques to analyze HR metrics and trends, including but not limited to employee turnover rates, performance evaluations, and diversity metrics. Generate reports and presentations to communicate findings to HR team members and stakeholders. 
Office Culture Enhancement: Collaborate with HR team members to develop and implement strategies to enhance office culture and team member engagement. Propose innovative ideas for team-building activities, recognition programs, and initiatives that promote a positive work environment. 
Recruitment Support: Assist with various aspects of the recruitment process, including candidate sourcing, resume screening, and interview coordination. Leverage data analysis to identify trends in candidate pipelines and recruitment strategies to optimize hiring outcomes. 
HR Administration: Provide administrative support to the HR team, including maintaining team member records, updating HR databases, and assisting with documentation related to team member benefits and policies. 
Project Support: Participate in special projects and initiatives as assigned by HR leadership, leveraging your analytical skills and understanding of office culture to contribute to the success of these projects. 
And other duties as assigned.

Currently pursuing a degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, Consulting, or a related field.
